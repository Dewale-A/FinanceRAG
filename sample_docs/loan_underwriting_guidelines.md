# Loan Underwriting Guidelines

## Purpose

These guidelines establish the standards and procedures for underwriting consumer and commercial loans. All lending decisions must comply with these guidelines, applicable regulations, and fair lending requirements.

## Regulatory Compliance

All underwriting must comply with:
- Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
- Fair Housing Act
- Truth in Lending Act (TILA)
- Real Estate Settlement Procedures Act (RESPA)
- Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA)
- Community Reinvestment Act (CRA)
- Dodd-Frank Act requirements

## Consumer Loan Products

### Personal Loans

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Minimum age: 18 years
- US citizen or permanent resident
- Valid Social Security Number
- Verifiable income source

**Credit Requirements:**
| Tier | Credit Score | Max DTI | Max Amount |
|------|-------------|---------|------------|
| Prime | 720+ | 40% | $50,000 |
| Near Prime | 680-719 | 36% | $35,000 |
| Non-Prime | 620-679 | 32% | $20,000 |

**Documentation Required:**
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of income (2 most recent pay stubs)
- Bank statements (2 months)
- Proof of residence

### Auto Loans

**Vehicle Requirements:**
- Maximum vehicle age: 7 years
- Maximum mileage: 100,000 miles
- Clean title (no salvage or flood)
- Acceptable makes and models per approved list

**Loan Terms:**
| Vehicle Age | Max Term | Max LTV |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| New | 84 months | 120% |
| 1-3 years | 72 months | 110% |
| 4-5 years | 60 months | 100% |
| 6-7 years | 48 months | 90% |

**Credit Requirements:**
- Minimum credit score: 600
- Maximum DTI: 45% (including proposed payment)
- Minimum monthly income: $1,500

### Mortgage Loans

**Conforming Loans (Fannie Mae/Freddie Mac eligible):**

| Product | Min Score | Max DTI | Max LTV | Max Loan |
|---------|-----------|---------|---------|----------|
| Purchase | 620 | 45% | 97% | $726,200 |
| Refinance | 640 | 43% | 95% | $726,200 |
| Cash-Out | 660 | 43% | 80% | $726,200 |

**Jumbo Loans:**
- Minimum credit score: 700
- Maximum DTI: 40%
- Maximum LTV: 80%
- Minimum reserves: 12 months PITI

**FHA Loans:**
- Minimum credit score: 580 (3.5% down) or 500 (10% down)
- Maximum DTI: 50% with compensating factors
- Mortgage insurance required
- Property must meet FHA standards

## Income Verification

### Employed Borrowers

**Required Documentation:**
- 2 years W-2 forms
- 30 days pay stubs
- Verbal verification of employment (within 10 days of closing)

**Income Calculation:**
- Base salary: Full amount
- Overtime: 2-year average (if consistent)
- Bonus: 2-year average
- Commission: 2-year average

### Self-Employed Borrowers

**Required Documentation:**
- 2 years personal tax returns
- 2 years business tax returns
- Year-to-date profit and loss statement
- Business license

**Income Calculation:**
```
Net Income = Taxable Income + Depreciation + Depletion - Business Use of Home
Average = (Year 1 + Year 2) / 2
```

Declining income over 2 years requires explanation and may require using lower year.

### Other Income Sources

| Income Type | Documentation | Continuance Required |
|-------------|---------------|---------------------|
| Social Security | Award letter | 3 years |
| Pension | Award letter | 3 years |
| Alimony | Divorce decree + 6 months receipt | 3 years |
| Rental Income | Tax returns + lease | 2 years history |
| Investment Income | Tax returns | 3 years |

## Debt-to-Income Calculation

### Front-End Ratio (Housing)
```
Housing DTI = (PITI + HOA + MI) / Gross Monthly Income
```

### Back-End Ratio (Total)
```
Total DTI = (Housing + All Monthly Debts) / Gross Monthly Income
```

### Debts Included:
- Proposed housing payment (PITI)
- Credit card minimum payments
- Auto loans
- Student loans (0.5% of balance if IBR)
- Personal loans
- Child support/alimony
- Other installment debts

### Debts Excluded:
- Debts with ≤10 payments remaining (except mortgage)
- Business debts (with documentation)
- Debts paid by others (with 12 months proof)

## Collateral Requirements

### Property Appraisal

**Appraisal Requirements:**
- Licensed/certified appraiser
- USPAP compliant
- Property inspection required
- Comparable sales within 12 months / 1 mile (urban) or 5 miles (rural)

**Appraisal Review Required When:**
- Value exceeds purchase price by >10%
- Value exceeds $1,000,000
- Property type is unusual
- Random quality control selection

### Title Insurance

Required for all mortgage loans:
- Lender's policy at minimum
- Owner's policy recommended
- Clear title required
- Acceptable exceptions only

## Risk-Based Pricing

### Interest Rate Factors

Base rate adjusted for:
| Factor | Adjustment |
|--------|------------|
| Credit Score <680 | +0.25% to +1.50% |
| LTV >80% | +0.125% to +0.50% |
| DTI >40% | +0.125% |
| Cash-out refinance | +0.125% to +0.375% |
| Investment property | +0.50% to +0.75% |
| 2-4 unit property | +0.25% to +0.50% |

### Loan Level Price Adjustments (LLPAs)

Per GSE matrices, additional adjustments apply based on:
- Credit score and LTV combination
- Loan purpose
- Property type
- Occupancy type

## Credit Exceptions

### Exception Types

**Policy Exception**: Deviation from standard guidelines
**Credit Exception**: One-time waiver with compensating factors

### Compensating Factors

Exceptions may be considered with:
- Significant liquid reserves (6+ months)
- Minimal payment increase
- Strong residual income
- Long employment tenure (5+ years)
- Low LTV (<70%)
- Excellent credit history (no lates ever)

### Exception Approval Authority

| Exception Level | Approval Authority |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Minor (single factor) | Senior Underwriter |
| Moderate (2 factors) | Credit Manager |
| Significant (3+ factors) | Credit Committee |
| Major (policy waiver) | Chief Credit Officer |

## Fair Lending

### Prohibited Factors

Credit decisions must not consider:
- Race, color, national origin
- Religion
- Sex, gender identity, sexual orientation
- Marital status
- Age (except for capacity)
- Receipt of public assistance
- Exercise of rights under CCPA

### Fair Lending Monitoring

- Second review required for denials
- Comparative file review quarterly
- Statistical analysis of approval rates
- Exception tracking by demographic

## Documentation Standards

### Credit File Requirements

Every file must contain:
- [ ] Signed application
- [ ] Credit report
- [ ] Income documentation
- [ ] Asset documentation
- [ ] Property documentation (if secured)
- [ ] Disclosures with signatures
- [ ] Decision documentation
- [ ] Conditions and clearing documentation

### Retention

- Approved loans: Life of loan + 7 years
- Denied applications: 7 years
- Withdrawn applications: 2 years
